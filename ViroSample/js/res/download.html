All parking at De Anza requires a paid fee or permit, except in spaces that are designated for disabilty parking or 30-minute visitor parking.

De Anza and Foothill College parking permits are valid on both campuses.

One-Day Permits

A one-day permit may be purchased for $3 at the yellow machines located in each student parking lot and the Flint Center and Stelling garages. The machines take nickels, dimes, quarters, dollar coins and dollar bills.

  • One-day permits are valid only in student lots.
  • For information on quarterly permits, see our Parking Permits webpage.

Disability Parking

Designated handicapped parking spaces are located in every parking lot at De Anza for cars with disability parking placards or license plates issued by the California Department of Motor Vehicles.

If you have a DMV disability placard or license plate, you don’t need to purchase a De Anza parking permit. You may park in any student or staff space, provided that your DMV placard is visible through your windshield.

Some disability parking spots are extra wide for the convenience of people using wheelchairs. Please don’t use these if you don’t have a wheelchair.

For more information, see our Disability Parking page.

Reserved Bike Parking

Visit our Bicycle Parking page to learn where to park on campus and how to sign up for a spot in the reserved bike parking corral.

Free Visitor Parking

Free parking for no more than 30 minutes is available in designated spaces in

  • Staff Lot A, near the Registration & Student Services Building 
  • Lot C, near the Physical Education Quad
